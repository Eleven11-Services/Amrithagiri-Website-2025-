In-depth discussions on spirituality and global peace

The webinar will feature profound conversations on the role of spirituality in shaping a peaceful world. Experts will share insights on how ancient wisdom and modern practices can come together to foster global harmony, personal well-being, and intercultural understanding.

Expert perspectives on Mahakumbh’s cultural and social impact

Mahakumbh is more than just a gathering; it is a centuries-old tradition that influences culture, society, and spiritual thought. Our distinguished speakers will delve into its historical significance, its role in bringing communities together, and how its teachings remain relevant in today's world.

Expert perspectives on Mahakumbh’s cultural and social impact

Professor Priyankar Upadhyaya is a renowned authority in peace and conflict studies, recently honored with the Amrithagiri Chair for World Peace by the Amrithagiri Research Foundation (ARF). His contributions transcend borders, shaping peace education at all levels. As the UNESCO Chair for Peace and Intercultural Understanding at Banaras Hindu University, he mentors future leaders, fostering intercultural harmony. His role as Senior Advisor at MIT World Peace University further amplifies his global influence in peacebuilding.

Interactive Q&A session

Participants will have the opportunity to engage directly with experts through a live Q&A session. This will be an open forum to discuss key themes, ask thought-provoking questions, and gain deeper insights into the connections between Mahakumbh, peace, and sustainable development.

A Global Collaboration for Peace

This webinar is a collaborative effort between Amrithagiri Research Foundation (ARF) and the UNESCO Chair for Peace, Banaras Hindu University (BHU), reflecting a shared commitment to fostering peace, spirituality, and sustainable progress. Amrithagiri Himalaya, known for its initiatives in spiritual tourism and global harmony, joins hands with UNESCO’s mission to promote peace education and intercultural dialogue.
