Services provided by Amrithagiri Himalaya

Amrithagiri is an important association of economic growth and development.

Villagers empowerment Programs
At Amrithagiri Himalaya, we believe in building self-reliant communities. Our villagers' empowerment programs focus on providing essential resources, training, and opportunities to help rural communities thrive.
Youth Skill-Building Intiatives
The youth hold the power to drive change. Our skill-building initiatives aim to equip young minds with technical skills, leadership training, and career guidance. We bridge the gap between potential and opportunity, ensuring every youth has a chance to excel.
Social Empowerment Programs
Our social empowerment programs are designed to address inequality and uplift marginalized groups. By fostering inclusivity, providing education, and promoting gender equality, we aim to create a society where every individual is valued and respected.
Environmental Intiatives
Protecting nature is at the heart of Amrithagiri Himalaya. Our environmental initiatives include tree plantation drives, conservation projects, and awareness campaigns. Together, we strive to preserve the natural beauty of the Himalayas for future generations.
Pilgrim Travel Assistance
Supporting spiritual journeys is a key part of our mission. Our pilgrim travel assistance services offer guidance, logistical support, and amenities to ensure a safe and enriching experience for those seeking solace in the serene Himalayan landscapes.
