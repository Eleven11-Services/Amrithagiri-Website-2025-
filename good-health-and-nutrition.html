AMRITHAGIRI HIMALAYA

Child Health

Amrithagiri Himalaya is deeply committed to addressing the health challenges faced by children in Himalayan villages, particularly female children, who often encounter unique barriers to healthcare. Recognizing that a healthy childhood is the foundation of a prosperous community, the organization focuses on improving child health through comprehensive initiatives tailored to the needs of these underserved regions.

AMRITHAGIRI HIMALAYA

Quality Education and
General Awareness

Amrithagiri Himalaya is dedicated to fostering quality education and raising general awareness, especially for female children in Himalayan villages. By addressing barriers to education and promoting awareness about its transformative power, the organization is empowering young minds to overcome social, cultural, and economic challenges.

AMRITHAGIRI HIMALAYA

Collaborations and Support

Amrithagiri Himalaya is committed to improving the health and educational outcomes of children in Himalayan villages through targeted initiatives. One such effort is the Health Checkup Camps in schools, aimed at addressing malnutrition while emphasizing the critical link between health and education. W planto partner with AIMS (Amrit Institute of Medical Sciences) and other healthcare institutions to ensure expert medical care during these camps.

Objective of Amrithagiri Himalaya

Amrithagiri Himalaya is a non-profit organization committed to preserving the natural wonders of the Himalayas and promoting sustainable living. We believe in the power of community-driven change and collaborate with local volunteers, environmental activists, and universities to create lasting positive impacts. Our initiatives range from tree planting and wildlife protection to education on eco-friendly practices. Join us on our mission to nurture the Earth and build a better tomorrow.
