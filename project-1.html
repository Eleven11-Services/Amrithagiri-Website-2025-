AMRITHAGIRI HIMALAYA

Ganga River

At Amrithagiri Himalaya, we aim to restore the Ganga River by addressing water contamination, preventing the disposal of dead bodies, reducing human waste dumping, and tackling eutrophication. Through eco-friendly filtration, sustainable sanitation, and awareness campaigns, we work with local communities and experts to revive this sacred river for a healthier environment. Join us in this vital mission.

Flora and Fauna

At Amrithagiri Himalaya, we are dedicated to protecting endangered species, preventing soil erosion, and conserving plant life. Through reforestation, sustainable land management practices, and wildlife protection efforts, we strive to restore biodiversity and maintain ecological balance. Our initiatives aim to ensure a healthier environment for future generations and support the preservation of our planet's natural resources.

Climate Change

Amrithagiri Himalaya is committed to raising awareness about disaster management through informative webinars. We encourage local supporters to actively participate by capturing images of villagers attending these sessions. Our goal is to empower communities with the knowledge and skills to effectively respond to disasters, ensuring their safety and resilience in the face of adversity.

Objective of Amrithagiri Himalaya

Amrithagiri Himalaya is a non-profit organization committed to preserving the natural wonders of the Himalayas and promoting sustainable living. We believe in the power of community-driven change and collaborate with local volunteers, environmental activists, and universities to create lasting positive impacts. Our initiatives range from tree planting and wildlife protection to education on eco-friendly practices. Join us on our mission to nurture the Earth and build a better tomorrow.
