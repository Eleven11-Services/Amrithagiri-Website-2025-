Priyankar Sir's Message on World Peace Day

+91 9400005707
info@amrithagirihimalaya.in

International Day of Peace, observed annually on September 21, was established by the UN in 1981 to unite humanity in promoting peace above differences and fostering a Culture of Peace worldwide. This year's theme emphasises that peace is active, shaping our choices, communities, and care for each other and the Earth. IAmid ongoing conflicts and climate crises in 2025, Peace Day serves as a vital reminder to act now—whether through advocacy, education, or community efforts—to create a ripple effect of kindness and harmony. Education plays a pivotal role in fostering peace. By integrating peace education into curricula, cultivating global citizenship, and leveraging technology, we empower individuals to build inclusive, understanding, and peaceful societies.Let us recommit to accessible, quality education as a cornerstone for a just, sustainable, and peaceful future. Together, our collective efforts can inspire meaningful change and promote harmony across the world.
