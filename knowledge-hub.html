Knowledge Hub

Knowledge creation and dissemination would  occupy the centrestage among the various activities of AH. knowledge is Power and precious too. AH would align with knowledge creators like universities to develop a robust knowledge hub for the ultimate benefit of the society and mankind. To create a robust Knowledge ecosystem for the ultimate benefit of the society, our nation and the world at large. Creating a strong Knowledge base aimed at contributing to the ARF through resource mobilisation is one of the goals of AH. The dream project of AH being the Amrithagiri Knowledge Bank.

Blogs

AH is a charitable NGO committed to serving humanity and the nation at large. We intend to provide information on contemporary spiritual and cultural events through our blogs through which many scholars, spiritualists and scholars would speak.

Publications

Integral Books and publications is an associate of AH .The publications include many books like Sri Ganesha-Significance of Divine Forms, The Self-A Biological Introduction To The BHAGAVAD-GITA (Volume-1), Power in Temples- A Modern Perspective, etc authored by the late ascetic and author Sri Srikanth. AH also intends to bring out periodicals and news letters which would carry stories and articles on Indian Philosophy, spirituality, Culture and allied subjects.

Insights
