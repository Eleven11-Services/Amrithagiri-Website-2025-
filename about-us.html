Amrithagiri Himalaya

Amrithagiri Himalaya is the flagship initiative of Amrithagiri Eco Life, a public charitable organization and registered NGO founded by Shri Denny Amrithagiri. Nestled in the serene landscapes of Uttarakhand, amidst the majestic Himalayas, Amrithagiri Himalaya is not just a sanctuary; it is a transformative movement focused on empowering Himalayan communities and promoting sustainable living. Guided by the eternal truths of Indian philosophy, the initiative seeks to nurture harmony between humanity and nature. Supported by a dedicated team of social reformers and environmentalists from the academic world, Amrithagiri Himalaya combines traditional wisdom with innovative approaches to create a future rooted in joy, unity, and ecological balance.

Our Vision

Lokah Samastah Sukhino Bhavantu
‍
At Amrithagiri,we envision a world where ancient Vedic knowledge coexist harmoniously with modern scientific knowledge. We aspire to create a global community of enlightened entrepreneurs,academics and students who are dedicated to preserving Hindu culture and tradition and promoting sustainable living. Believing in the philosophy of Vasudeva kudumbakom we aim to bring happiness to the whole world. Let us save Sanatana Dharma for preserving plurality, peace and prosperity for posterity everywhere.

Our Mission

At Amrithagiri, we integrate timeless Vedic principles with modern innovation to build a global community rooted in universal values, sustainable living, and well-being. By promoting Sanatana Dharma (eternal truth and the science of life) through education, research, and community development, we aim to create a harmonious and pluralistic world for future generations.

Guided by the ideals of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam ("The world is one family") and Loka Samastha Sukhino Bhavantu ("May all beings be happy"), we foster inclusivity, unity, and respect beyond the boundaries of religion, nationality, or culture, envisioning a peaceful and harmonious world for humanity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the goal of Amrithagiri Himalaya?

Amrithagiri Himalaya's goal is to empower and uplift village communities through education, sustainable living practices, and a deep connection to Indian philosophy. Led by Denny Amrithagiri, the ashram aims to create a world where joy, unity, and love flourish for all, with a focus on transforming lives and fostering harmony with nature and society. The mission also emphasizes the preservation of traditional wisdom while integrating it into modern practices for holistic development.

Where's Amrithagiri Himalaya located?

Amrithagiri Himalaya is situated at Mandogi, Uttarakhand.

What is Amrithagiri Himalaya's impact on people?

Amrithagiri Himalaya has a profound impact on people by empowering village communities through education, sustainable living practices, Ayurveda, and a deep connection to Indian philosophy. It transforms lives by fostering personal growth, unity, and harmony with nature. The ashram integrates Ayurvedic healing, promoting holistic well-being and natural health practices that enhance physical, mental, and spiritual wellness. Through this blend of ancient wisdom and modern practices, Amrithagiri Himalaya helps individuals develop self-reliance, live in balance with the environment, and experience joy, peace, and purpose, creating a ripple effect of positive change in the community.

Why Amrithagiri Himalaya is needed?

Amrithagiri Himalaya is needed to address the lack of education, sustainable living, and holistic health in rural communities. By empowering individuals through education, Ayurveda, and Indian philosophy, it helps preserve traditions while fostering modern development. It offers a sanctuary for growth, promoting harmony with nature and well-being, and inspires positive change in both individuals and communities.

How will Amrithagiri Himalaya accomplish their goals?

Amrithagiri Himalaya will accomplish its goals by empowering village communities through education, sustainable living practices, and the integration of Ayurveda and Indian philosophy. The ashram will provide resources, training, and guidance to help individuals become self-reliant, improve their health, and live harmoniously with nature. By blending ancient wisdom with modern solutions, it will create a model for holistic living that fosters personal and collective growth, inspiring positive change in communities and beyond.
