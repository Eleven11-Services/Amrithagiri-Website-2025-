Academic Alliances

Amrithagiri Research Foundation has forged strong academic alliances with some of India’s most prestigious research institutions, promoting collaborative efforts in the fields of agriculture, history, and environmental science. Key academic partnerships include:
‍
1. Indian Council for Agricultural Research (ICAR)
2. Indian Agricultural Research Institute (IARI)
3. Indian Council for Historical Research (ICHR)
‍
‍Various other renowned research institutionsThese partnerships enable the foundation to leverage cutting-edge research and innovations, ensuring the upliftment of communities through sustainable practices and knowledge exchange.

Research Grants and Fellowships

Amrithagiri Research Foundation is committed to nurturing the next generation of scholars and researchers. By offering research grants and fellowships, the foundation supports students and academics focused on capacity building and the development of groundbreaking research projects. The aim is to empower young minds, enhance their skills, and encourage innovative solutions that align with the foundation's core mission of sustainable living and cultural preservation.

Objective of Amrithagiri Himalaya

Amrithagiri Himalaya is a non-profit organization committed to preserving the natural wonders of the Himalayas and promoting sustainable living. We believe in the power of community-driven change and collaborate with local volunteers, environmental activists, and universities to create lasting positive impacts. Our initiatives range from tree planting and wildlife protection to education on eco-friendly practices. Join us on our mission to nurture the Earth and build a better tomorrow.
