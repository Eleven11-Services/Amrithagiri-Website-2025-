Programs We Offer

Backbone of Amrithagiri Himalaya is our allies.

1. Wildlife Conservation Projects

Protecting native species through habitat restoration and anti-poaching efforts.

2. Community Forest Management

Encouraging locals to sustainably manage and protect forest resources.

3. Renewable Energy Solutions

Promoting solar, wind, and biogas technologies in villages to reduce dependency on non-renewable resources.

4. Eco-Friendly Infrastructure

Building sustainable homes, schools, and community centers using green materials and techniques.

Preserving Beauty of Himalaya

Amrithagiri Himalaya is deeply committed to preserving the natural beauty of the Himalayas and ensuring its sustainability for generations to come. Through innovative and community-driven environmental initiatives, Amrithagiri Himalaya safeguards the region’s ecological balance, ensuring a harmonious coexistence between nature and humanity.
