About Us

Nestled in the majestic Himalayas, Amrithagiri Himalaya is more than just an ashram - it’s a movement led by Shri Denny Thomas Amrithagiri. Here, along the sacred banks of the Ganga, we are transforming lives by empowering Himalayan village communities with education and sustainable living practices. Guided by the eternal truths of Indian philosophy, Amrithagiri is on a mission to create a world where joy, unity, and love flourish for all. Step into a journey that blends tradition with innovation, and be part of a legacy that nurtures both people and planet.
